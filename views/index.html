About HOG

Welcome to House of Greens! Founded during the first wave of the COVID-19 pandemic, we are a hyperlocal indoor farm dedicated to providing fresh and flavorful leafy greens. Our story begins with Karan, the founder of House of Greens, a passionate civil engineer with a love for both enjoying and growing delicious food. Inspired by his desire to bring the goodness of fresh greens to the community, Karan embarked on a mission to create a sustainable and accessible source of locally grown produce. With a deep understanding of the importance of quality and taste, Karan has cultivated a diverse range of exotic leafy greens in our indoor farm. At House of Greens, we believe that everyone deserves access to fresh and healthy food, and we are committed to delivering the freshest greens directly to your table. Join us on this exciting journey of flavor, health, and sustainable farming practices as we grow together, one leafy green at a time.
